A Fully Automated
Digital Campus

DIUa1 is not an ordinary management system. It is a fully automated digital campus that unifies Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, and Blockchain technology to transform the conventional challenges of university life into a data-driven smart campus experience.

EXECUTIVE VISION

The core goal of this system is to make every day-to-day university task simpler, faster, and fully automated for both faculty and students — so teachers spend more time teaching, and students spend more time learning.

At the heart of the entire ecosystem is OBE (Outcome-Based Education). All other modules — admissions, transport, finance, and security — serve as the supporting pillars of this ecosystem.

02 — ADMISSION

Smart Admission System

A seamless and modern admission process designed for incoming students.

Tesseract OCR Google Vision AI RAG + LLM Three.js WebGL Blockchain
  • Auto Admission Form Fill-Up
    Upload a photo of your National ID or certificate and within 2–3 seconds, your name, roll number, GPA, and date of birth are automatically populated into the form.
  • AI Career Architect
    A smart multi-step quiz powered by RAG architecture recommends the most suitable department. A career flowchart is displayed alongside every suggestion.
  • Virtual Campus Tour
    An interactive 3D campus map built on a WebGL-powered Three.js engine. Clicking any building reveals detailed information about that department.
  • Cheat-Free Entrance Examination
    Powered by the Page Visibility API and AI proctoring. Switching tabs or minimising the screen sends a log to the server and displays a red warning on screen.
  • Blockchain Payment Ledger
    Every transaction is hashed and permanently stored on a private blockchain. A green "Verified Block" tick mark confirms successful payment.
  • Smart Hall Management
    A real-time seat availability dashboard and a fully digital room allotment system for residential halls.
03 — TEACHER PORTAL

Automated Teacher Portal

Reducing teachers' administrative burden so they can focus on academic quality.

Face Recognition Speech-to-Text NLP Grading XGBoost KPI
  • AI-Vision Passive Attendance
    In-class cameras use face recognition to automatically sync attendance to the portal. No manual action is required from the teacher.
  • Visual Mark Recognition
    Simply hold answer scripts in front of the camera. The system reads the Student ID and handwritten marks, then instantly populates the grade sheet in real time.
  • Voice-to-Excel Grading Agent
    The teacher simply calls out marks — "15, 18, 12, 20" — and the agent automatically enters them against the correct roll numbers in Excel. Commands like "skip" and "correct" are also supported.
  • Class Comprehension Dashboard
    Based on student feedback, the dashboard shows how well each topic was understood, categorised into three levels. It also provides suggestions for the next class.
  • Voice Command System
    Submit leave applications, create notices, generate reports, and reschedule classes — all by voice command alone.
  • KPI Tracking & Auto Reward
    A regression model analyses long-term performance patterns and assigns ratings of "Excellent", "Average", or "Needs Improvement". Human approval remains mandatory for any formal escalation.
  • Mental Health Support
    An LLM-powered chatbot that responds with empathy. A built-in safety classifier forwards crisis situations to a human counsellor.

09–10 — FINANCE & TRANSPORT

Transparent Finance & Safe Commuting

Blockchain-backed transactions and a real-time tracking system.

Hyperledger Smart Contract TOTP QR WebSocket GPS
  • Financial Credit Score
    An AI model analyses payment timing and displays a score in a speedometer-style ring. A higher score unlocks access to EMI (instalment) payment facilities.
  • Blockchain Ledger
    Every transaction is saved to a private blockchain via smart contracts. The system is completely tamper-proof and free from any possibility of fraud.
  • Bilingual Finance Chatbot
    Type a query in any language — for example, "When is my last payment due?" — and receive an answer within a second.
  • Dynamic TOTP QR Bus Entry
    A QR code that regenerates every 30 seconds. The TOTP algorithm syncs with the student's ID to produce a fresh, secure QR code for each boarding.
  • Live GPS Tracking
    Live location data is streamed from the GPS module to the server via WebSocket. The bus icon moves in real time on the map displayed in the app.
  • Automated Parental Notification
    As soon as the QR code is scanned, a text message with a tracking link is sent to the parent's phone — for example: "Nafisa boarded Bus Surjo at 8:00 AM." A notification is also sent when the student alights.
05 — STUDENT PORTAL

Smart Student Portal

A complete digital hub for students' everyday academic needs and career guidance.

AI Assistant Smart Notifications Career Roadmap Library RAG
  • Smart Cover Page Generator
    Upload a photo of an assignment and a professional cover page is instantly generated using the student's profile data — name, ID, and semester — with no manual input required.
  • Intelligent Library & Research Hub
    Books, e-books, and research papers are recommended based on the student's curriculum and current semester. A "Recommended for You" slider is displayed on the dashboard.
  • Daily Learning Feedback Dashboard
    After each class, students can rate their comprehension — "Understood well", "Partially understood", or "Did not understand at all". This feedback is immediately sent to the teacher's class comprehension dashboard.
  • Career & Job Guidance
    Detailed guidance tailored to specific job sectors. A complete career roadmap shows students from each department the various paths available to them.
  • Smart Routine & Notifications
    Automatic notifications are sent the day before an exam or 10 minutes before a class. Lab sessions, quiz dates, and final exam schedules are updated automatically.
  • AI Assistant
    Instant assistance for university policies, hall information, and any academic query. Automatic replies and solutions are provided for all general questions.
06–08 — NEXUS HUB & COMMUNITY

Clubs, Concierge & Review System

The university's social presence, community services, and staff review system — all in one place.

NLP Sentiment Gig Economy GPS Directory Verified Feed
  • Student Club Directory
    An interactive directory covering all clubs including the Robotics Club, Computer Programming Club, and Cultural Club. Each club's page includes links to Facebook, LinkedIn, and Discord, a mission statement, a membership link, and announcements for upcoming events.
  • Verified Social Feed
    A centralised link-tree of the university's official Threads, LinkedIn, and Facebook pages. Students receive information only from trusted sources, eliminating the spread of rumours.
  • Campus Assist Gig Economy
    Using their verified ID, students can help one another with small tasks — such as deliveries or fetching items. Students earn an income while receiving safe services within a familiar environment.
  • Nearby Restaurant Directory
    A curated list of eateries near campus, complete with ratings, reviews, and GPS locations. Includes food and cafeteria order tracking and price monitoring.
  • University Staff Review System
    Students can review office staff, canteen workers, librarians, and security guards. An NLP Sentiment Analysis model processes the reviews and displays results on the admin dashboard — for example: "Library Staff Karim: 90% Positive Feedback."
  • AI-Powered Smart Notice Board
    Personalised push notifications based on each student's profile and interests. A CSE student receives notifications about CSE seminars; a BBA student receives updates about business competitions.
  • Local News & Campus Update Board
    An AI agent automatically collects relevant news and sends it via push notification — for example: "There will be no electricity from 6 PM tonight" or "The road ahead is closed due to a procession." Students can filter updates by their area and interests.
11 — PARENT PORTAL

Comprehensive Parent Portal

A fully dedicated portal for parents — all information about their child in one place.

Real-Time Updates Blockchain Verified Live GPS SMS Alerts
  • Academic Oversight
    View exam results, grade trends, and attendance records in real time. Subjects in which the child is struggling are displayed in graphical form.
  • Class Faculty Directory
    The name, photo, and contact details of the child's class teacher and every subject teacher are accessible directly from the portal.
  • Financial Oversight & Fee Management
    Real-time balance, upcoming payment dates, and amounts can be viewed in advance. A verified payment history drawn from the blockchain ledger is available, along with real-time spending notifications for the child.
  • Transport Monitoring
    View the live location of the bus. An instant SMS notification with a timestamp and GPS location is sent whenever the child boards or alights.
  • Local Area Updates
    Relevant news from around the campus — road closures, power outages, or special events. Parents can plan their child's commute well in advance.
12 — GOVERNANCE & SECURITY

Institutional Governance & Campus Security

Strengthening administrative capability and ensuring the overall safety of the campus.

Computer Vision CCTV Forecasting Model IoT Energy Blockchain Proof
  • Decision Support System
    Forecasting models are used to generate budget plans and admission forecasts for the upcoming semester, helping the administration make well-informed, data-driven decisions.
  • AI Surveillance
    A Computer Vision model analyses the CCTV feed to detect crowds or physical altercations. Upon detecting anything unusual, a red bounding box is drawn around the area and an "Alert: Crowd Detected" notification is triggered. Evidence of this surveillance is stored on the blockchain.
  • AI Sentiment Analysis
    NLP processes student reviews to automatically analyse and generate reports on the quality of staff, drivers, and cafeteria services — all presented on the admin dashboard.
  • Smart Energy Control
    An AI predictive model uses data from IoT motion and temperature sensors to make decisions. When a classroom is empty, lights and air conditioning are switched off automatically. The Energy Dashboard shows the total units of electricity saved each day.
  • Smart Expense Transparency Dashboard
    A visible dashboard showing exactly where and how much of the university's funds are being spent — completely transparent to both the administration and students.

IOT
Motion + Temp Sensors
